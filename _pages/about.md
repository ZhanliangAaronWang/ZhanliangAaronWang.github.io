Welcome to my homepage! My name is Zhanliang (Aaron) Wang, currently pursuing a Master of Arts in Applied Mathematics and Computational Science (AMCS) at the University of Pennsylvania. I will begin my Ph.D. in the same program in Fall 2025, under the supervision of Dr. Kai Wang from the Children's Hospital of Philadelphia (CHOP) and the Perelman School of Medicine. I completed my Bachelor of Science in Applied Mathematics at New York University with Summa Cum Laude.

My research insterests include Large Language Models (LLMs) and Multimodality Models (MMLs), particularly in the context of precise genetic disease diagnosis. I am exploring the application of Reinforcement Learning in healthcare field, aiming to develop AI systems that improce decision-making and patient outcomes.
